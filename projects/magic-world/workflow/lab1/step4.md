### 版本一：角色定稿圖（Elwyn Character Design Sheet）
這個版本適合用來生成角色的全身或半身定稿，重點在於展示她的整體造型、服裝細節與「精緻」的畫風。

#### 提示詞 (English)：
> **A complete anime character design reference sheet for a female Elf Mage, Elwyn.** High quality anime art, hand-drawn traditional cel-shading style. **Character details:** She has very long, wavy, ash-blonde hair tied loosely, silver double eyes with a gentle but distant expression, and long pointed elven ears with a soft pink blush. She wears a loose, flowy, deep teal magician's robe with silver embroidery and a hood (currently down). Underneath is a cream-colored soft linen tunic. She is holding a twisted wooden staff with a pale blue, glowing magic gem set at the top. On her belt are small potion bottles and an antique spellbook. **Style:** Soft textured line art (sepia or dark warm gray lines, not sharp black), soft watercolor background, breathable atmosphere. Skin has a pearlescent, soft-touch translucency. The coloring is soft-cel, low saturation but high brightness. The lighting is soft ambient daylight. Studio Ghibli vibes with Shinkai Makoto lighting detail, refined and cute. High resolution, detailed.

#### 提示詞摘要 (中文參考)：
> 艾露溫的完整動畫角色設計參考圖。高品質動畫美術，手繪賽璐璐風格。**角色細節：** 亞麻色長捲髮散亂繫著，銀色雙瞳表情溫柔而淡漠，精靈長耳帶有淡淡粉紅。身穿寬鬆、飄逸的深青色法師袍，上面有銀色刺繡，兜帽垂在背後。內穿奶油色柔軟亞麻襯衫。手持頂端鑲嵌著淡藍色發光寶石的扭曲木杖。腰帶上掛著小藥瓶和古董魔導書。**風格：** 帶紋理的柔和線條（棕色線），柔和水彩背景，有呼吸感。皮膚透亮柔軟。上色為柔和賽璐璐，低飽和高明度。環境光。吉卜力氛圍加上新海誠式的光影細節，精緻可愛。

### 版本二：劇本日常 - 慵懶漂浮（Scene 2: Lazy Floating）
這個版本根據你的劇本第2場景設計，重點在於捕捉艾露溫「懶散」、「優雅」以及用魔法戲耍蝴蝶的動作。

#### 提示詞 (English)：
> **A soft-styled anime still featuring the female Elf Mage Elwyn, as seen in a fantasy journey film.** Hand-drawn art style, high refinement. **Action:** Elwyn is effortlessly floating about 30cm off the ground, lying horizontally and leaning comfortably back against her magic staff, as if resting on a cloud. She is yawning, her hand covering her mouth, with a small teardrop at the corner of her eye. With her other index finger, a tiny magical blue particle trail is playfully leading a beautiful butterfly around in a circle. Her ash-blonde hair and deep teal magician's robe flow gently in the light breeze, emphasizing her weightlessness. **Background:** A winding mountain path at sunrise, soft pink and gold cloud-sea below, distant snowy peaks. Watercolor textured background with realistic light bloom and atmosphere. Sepia-toned line art, warm lighting, Ghibli style, detailed and cure.

#### 提示詞摘要 (中文參考)：
> 艾露溫在奇幻旅程電影中的柔和風格劇照。手繪美術，精緻度高。**動作：** 艾露溫正輕鬆地在離地 30 公分處水平漂浮，慵懶地靠在她的魔法杖上，就像休息在雲朵上。她正在打呵欠，手遮著嘴，眼角帶淚。另一隻食指尖正用一小段發光的藍色魔法粒子，頑皮地引導著一隻美麗的蝴蝶繞圈。她的亞麻色頭髮和深青色法師袍在微風中緩緩飄動，強調了她的無重力感。**背景：** 日出時分彎曲的山徑，下方是柔和的粉色與金色雲海，遠處是雪山巔峰。水彩紋理背景，具有真實的光暈和空氣感。棕色線條，暖色光，吉卜力風格，細緻可愛。
