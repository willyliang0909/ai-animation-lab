# 角色生成提示詞


😊
```
給我兩位主要人物的定裝照生成提示詞
```

## note
> 整體沒大問題

🤖
```
為了確保動畫視覺的一致性，這兩位人物的定裝照需要有鮮明的對比：主角（我）代表「活生生、有溫度的人氣」，而韻如則要呈現出「如紙般單薄、蒼白且缺乏生氣」的靈異感。

以下是為你設計的 AI 定裝照生成提示詞：

1. 主角：從國外回港的女孩 (The Protagonist)
視覺核心： 健康的膚色、帶著旅行的疲憊感、穿搭現代、具有強烈的人類感。

AI 提示詞：

Prompt: A full-body studio portrait of a young Hong Kong woman in her mid-20s, back from abroad. She has a natural, healthy skin tone with a hint of warmth. Her expression is curious but slightly tired. Wearing a modern casual outfit: a light beige trench coat over a simple denim shirt, carrying a functional backpack. Her hair is messy-chic, medium length. Realistic lighting, high-quality skin texture, 8k, cinematic photography, neutral background.

2. 室友：韻如 (Yun-Ru, The Ghostly Roommate)
視覺核心： 「白得像紙」、「瘦削」、「清秀但空洞」。她的美感應該帶有一種讓人不安的冷冽。

AI 提示詞：

Prompt: A hauntingly beautiful studio portrait of "Yun-Ru," a 20-year-old female Chinese university student. She has extremely pale, porcelain-like skin and an exceptionally slender, frail physique. Her features are delicate and "clean" (清秀), with no makeup. She has long, straight, jet-black hair that contrasts sharply with her skin. Her eyes are dark and slightly vacant. Wearing a simple, oversized white cotton dress that makes her look even thinner. "London Fog" grey lighting, minimalist Nordic aesthetic, eerie and silent atmosphere, hyper-realistic, 8k.

動畫師調色建議 (Style Guide)：
膚色對比： 在後製時，請務必將主角的膚色調為偏暖橘/肉色，而韻如的膚色則要調成帶點青藍色調的死白，這能讓觀眾下意識感覺到兩者「陰陽兩隔」的生理差異。

光影處理： * 主角的光影要有層次，有明顯的影子。

韻如的光影可以刻意處理得**「平」**一些，讓她看起來像是一個剪影或是一個沒有體積感的紙人，呼應她爬樓梯時極度輕鬆、沒有重量的詭異特徵。
```
