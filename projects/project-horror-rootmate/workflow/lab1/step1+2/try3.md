這是一個非常棒的調整建議。**第一人稱視角（POV）**能極大化靈異故事的代入感，讓聽眾不僅是「看」故事，而是「受困」在主角的感官裡。

【腳本】

主角（內心獨白）：家人都在國外，我想回香港找個老朋友，但我得先有個落腳的地方。

動作：主角的視線從喧鬧的街景，移到一張手寫的告示上。

對話（回憶）：電話那頭的聲音細細的，很柔和。「你好，請問是找人合租嗎？」

Prompt: POV shot, first-person perspective, looking at a weathered, yellowish hand-written flyer taped to a rusty electric pole in a crowded Hong Kong street. The flyer says "Room for Rent" in traditional Chinese. Gritty urban atmosphere, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, 1990s Hong Kong style, grainy texture, 4k.

Prompt: POV shot, looking up at a pale, slender woman standing on a dark, narrow staircase of an old Hong Kong tenement building. She is turning her head slightly back to look directly into the camera with a mysterious, hollow expression. Dim, creepy lighting, peeling green paint on walls, cinematic horror atmosphere, hyper-realistic, high contrast shadows.

畫面三：定局（踏入凶屋）
